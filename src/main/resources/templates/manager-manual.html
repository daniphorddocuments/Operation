Getting Started

Welcome to the Mahanga Manager Portal. This guide will help you master all the features and tools available to you.

1.1 Accessing Your Dashboard

After logging in with your manager credentials, you'll be taken directly to your shop dashboard. Here you can see:

  • Quick overview of today's sales
  • Product inventory status
  • Staff on duty
  • Pending notifications and alerts

1.2 Dashboard Layout

The manager dashboard is organized into several key areas:

  • Top Bar: Displays your name, current date/time, and quick actions
  • Sidebar: Main navigation menu for all features
  • Main Content Area: Detailed information and task management
  • Quick Stats: Key performance metrics at a glance

1.3 First-Time Setup

Before you start using the system:

  1. Verify your shop information is correct
  2. Set up your staff members
  3. Add your products to the system
  4. Configure your pricing
  5. Review your settings
💡 Tip: Take a screenshot of this manual or download the PDF for offline reference.

Shop Management

Manage your shop operations, information, and team.

2.1 View Shop Information

To view or update your shop details:

  • Click the shop name in the top navigation
  • Review general information: name, location, contact
  • Update shop details as needed
  • Click Save to confirm changes

2.2 Managing Staff

View all employees assigned to your shop:

  • Navigate to the Staff section
  • View all employee details and roles
  • See who's currently working
  • View performance metrics per employee
  • Communicate with staff through the system

2.3 Shop Performance

Monitor your shop's performance with real-time metrics:

  • Daily sales total
  • Transaction count
  • Average transaction value
  • Top selling products
  • Inventory status
📊 Pro Tip: Review daily performance metrics every morning to set team goals.

Product Management

Add, update, and manage your shop's product inventory.

3.1 Adding New Products

To add a product to your shop:

  • 1Click on "Add Product" button
  • 2Enter product name
  • 3Select category (e.g., Electronics, Clothing)
  • 4Set the selling price
  • 5Enter initial quantity in stock
  • 6Add product description (optional)
  • 7Click "Save" to confirm

3.2 Editing Products

To modify an existing product:

  • Find the product in the product list
  • Click the product name to open details
  • Make desired changes
  • Click "Update" to save changes

3.3 Managing Inventory

Keep track of product quantities:

  • View current stock levels in the Products section
  • Update quantities as inventory changes
  • The system automatically adjusts after each sale
  • Monitor products with low stock
  • Plan restocking based on trends
⚠️ Important: Regularly update inventory to prevent overselling and maintain accuracy.

Sales Management

Record and manage all sales transactions through your shop.

4.1 Recording a Sale

To process a new sale:

  • 1Click "New Sale" from the main menu
  • 2Select customer (existing or create new)
  • 3Add products to the cart
  • 4Set quantities for each product
  • 5Review the total amount
  • 6Select payment method
  • 7Process payment and complete sale

4.2 Payment Methods

The system supports multiple payment methods:

  • Cash payment
  • Mobile money (M-Pesa)
  • Bank transfer
  • Credit/Debit card
  • Credit on account (if approved)

4.3 Sales Receipt

After completing a sale, you can:

  • View the receipt on screen
  • Print the receipt for the customer
  • Email receipt to customer (if available)
  • Save receipt reference for future reference
✓ Tip: Keep receipts organized for easy reference and auditing purposes.

Reports & Analytics

Analyze your shop's performance through comprehensive reports.

5.1 Available Reports

  • Daily Sales Report: Summary of all sales for a specific day
  • Weekly Summary: Week-over-week comparison and trends
  • Monthly Performance: Complete monthly analysis
  • Product Performance: Top sellers and low movers
  • Staff Performance: Sales metrics per employee

5.2 Generating Reports

To generate a report:

  • Navigate to the Reports section
  • Select report type
  • Choose date range
  • Apply filters (optional)
  • Click "Generate"
  • View, print, or download report

5.3 Using Report Data

Use reports to:

  • Identify top-performing products
  • Monitor sales trends
  • Evaluate staff performance
  • Plan inventory and restocking
  • Set realistic sales targets
  • Make data-driven business decisions
📈 Strategy: Review reports weekly to identify trends and adjust strategy accordingly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions. You'll receive a reset link via email.

Q: How do I record a product return?

A: Create a negative sale transaction for the returned products. Go to "New Sale" and mark it as a return to adjust inventory accordingly.

Q: What should I do if I make a mistake in a sale?

A: Contact your administrator to void the transaction. Always verify details before completing a sale to prevent errors.

Q: How often should I reconcile my sales?

A: Reconcile daily at the end of your shift and weekly for more detailed analysis. Monthly reconciliation is mandatory.

Q: Can I edit a completed sale?

A: Most sales cannot be edited once completed. Create a return and new sale if correction is needed.
